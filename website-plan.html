The site provides information about the company, but more important than that is the product information, includingname, origin, price and other related information.

Color Schema

  • Accent Color: Gold (#bfa046) - Used for buttons, highlights, and call-to-action elements.
  • Background: Light gray (#f9f9f9) - Used for page background.
